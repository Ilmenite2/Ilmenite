About Ilmenite

Where Tradition Meets Innovation

Founded on the principles of excellence and sophistication, Ilmenite represents the perfect fusion of old money elegance and modern innovation. Our name, derived from the precious titanium-iron oxide mineral, reflects our commitment to rare quality and enduring value.

We believe in creating experiences that transcend the ordinary—whether through our meticulously crafted software solutions, our curated luxury merchandise, or our thoughtful approach to investment guidance. Every aspect of our company is designed to embody the timeless sophistication of established wealth while embracing the possibilities of tomorrow.

Our Founder

Ilmenite was founded on April 19, 2023, by San (born May 8, 2008)—a visionary entrepreneur who embodies the next generation of luxury business leadership. As a teen founder, San recognized the opportunity to bridge traditional wealth-building principles with modern digital innovation, creating a company that honors the sophistication of old money while pioneering new frontiers in technology, luxury goods, and investment guidance.

This unique perspective—combining youthful innovation with timeless elegance—defines everything we do at Ilmenite, ensuring our offerings remain both cutting-edge and classically refined.
